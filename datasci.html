About This Course

This course is designed for beginners who want to learn the basics of the Hindi language. We will cover simple vocabulary, basic grammar, and speaking skills to get you started.

  • Natural, day-to-day expressions to use in Hindi Conversation
  • A range of Hindi vocabulary for different conversational contexts
  • The key grammar you need to talk about past, present, and future with confidence
  • Hindi Pronunciation tips to sound more like a native
  • How to introduce yourself and initiate conversation in Hindi speaking
  • How to respond appropriately to what others say in Hindi speaking
  • How to deal with tricky situations and end Hindi conversations politely
  • Functional Hindi language for restaurants, travel, talking on the phone, etc.
  • Tips about cultural differences and expectations in Hindi speaking
